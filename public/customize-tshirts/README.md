This folder contains images for the customizable t-shirt products.

11. `crop-pink.jpg` - Crop Top Tee (Pink)
12. `tank-black.jpg` - Tank Top (Black)

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Dimensions**: 800x800px or higher (square aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Under 500KB for optimal loading
- **Background**: Clean white or transparent background preferred
- **Quality**: High resolution, clear product view

## Temporary Placeholder

## How to Add Images

1. Take or source high-quality photos of each t-shirt style
2. Edit images to have consistent dimensions and background
3. Optimize images for web (compress without losing quality)
4. Name files exactly as listed above
5. Place them in this directory: `/public/customize-tshirts/`

## Tips for Product Photography

- Use natural lighting or softbox lighting
- Photograph on a mannequin or flat lay
- Ensure consistent angle and framing for all products
- Show the t-shirt clearly without distractions
- Consider showing front and back views
